OUR PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

Following are our products and services that we provide to our clients to meet their Fire, Health and Safety needs.

PRODUCTS AND SERVINCES

  • Supply and Installation of Fire Extinguishers and Safety Equipment in Office Buildings, Houses, Trucks and Ships.
  • Supply and Installation of Fire Hose Reel with Fire Blankets.
  • Supply and Installation of Fire Safety Signs.
  • Supply and Installation of Fire Smoke Detectors in Offices and Homes.
  • Supply and Installation of Fire Alarm Systems (Setup to be made with the clients phone so a SMS notification will be receive when fire and smoke is detected in the office or house).
  • Servicing of Fire Extinguishers and Hose Reels.
  • Refilling of Fire Extinguishers.

  • Training in Applying First Aid

  • Driver Trainings

Other Services
